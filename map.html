About the map

The seafloor, shaded honestly.

The default basemap is the GEBCO 2024 Grid — the global, authoritative bathymetric compilation maintained by the IOC and IHO. For European seas (Black Sea, North Sea, Mediterranean) you can overlay the EMODnet Bathymetry DTM at ~115 m resolution from the top-right layer switcher — it sharpens the German Bight and Balearic shelves in particular.

The four station coordinates currently displayed are approximate regional placeholders; exact field-recorded GPS values will replace them as each project is published. Each marker links through to its full project page on this site.
